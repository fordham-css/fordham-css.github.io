Coding Nights

Our weekly open coding sessions are the heart and soul of CSS
Wednesdays @ 7pm | Fordham Foundry

We're kicking off the year strong with our first coding session of the semester. Our first meeting will focus on Github and other essential tools for developers. Beginners are more than welcome, just bring a laptop and a craving to build!

Computing Sciences Society's open coding nights are casual sessions geared towards collaborative working and helping each other out when we get stuck. We're here to help you leverage the technology out there to make your ideas come to life. Of course, you can also use the awesome space as a great environment for a personal project, classwork, or self-study Feel free to come and go as you please, but be prepared to leave with some (epic) stickers and more than a few inspired ideas.

Local Hack Day

Fordham University's FIRST hackathon!
Saturday, December 6th 9am-9pm | Fordham Foundry

Local Hack Day is your chance to build that great idea you have in the back of your head and make it a reality. It's an opportunity for students around the world to come together and hack.

Want to learn how to code? A hack day is the perfect chance to work with your fellow classmates to learn how. Additionally, we'll be streaming workshops all throughout the event. You can build anything you want, just make sure to bring any hardware, laptops, or smartphones that you want to utilize during the event.

Never been to a hackathon? This is the perfect chance for you to experience what a hackathon is like. Regardless of experience level, all are welcome to learn, celebrate and build.

Sponsored by Major League Hacking, Evernote, and Healthy Fresh

3D Printing w/ Dr. Stephen Holler

Come join us for a talk on the explosive technology of additive manufacturing
Thursday, November 6th 1pm | Freeman 105

Dr. Holler of the Physics department will be joining us for a talk on the history of this disruptive tech, as well as a hands on demonstration on how designing and printing an object works with a live 3D printer!

Can't make it? No worries! Come out to our Wednesday coding sessions and we'll be demoing one for a closer hands on look. BUT, you will be walking away with a 3D printed object of your own if you attend so be sure to come out for this great talk!
