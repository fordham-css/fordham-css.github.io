    The research and development arm of Fordham Computer Science

What we do

We're here to help, learn and explore!

Research and Development

We are working on special projects in hardware, software, engineering and data science that stem from pet projects to potential products.

Events and lectures

We host alumni and tech professionals in awesome lectures here on campus. We also have workshops, meetups, and hackathons!

Tutoring & consulting

Need help? We'll put you in touch with the CS tutoring center. Need help with your own project? Talk to us!

Open Labs

We have the tools, resources, and experience to help develop students' bold ideas into feasible and buildable projects.

Our Events

What we have done so far

HackNY

To close out the year, we led a group to participate in a 24 hour hackathon at Columbia University.

Personal Sites

In this interactive workshop, members learned the basic setup of LinkedIn and their own personal website.

Alumni Speaker

Last fall, a CS alumnus paid us a visit to chat and share his insight on his career path after Fordham.

CSS Social

To kickoff last year, we grabbed some pizza, dusted off the N64, and tried out new hardware in our first club social.
