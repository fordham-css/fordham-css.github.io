HackRPI

Join us November 14-15 for a hackathon at RPI!

CSS is going to HackRPI! HackRPI is a hackathon that will be held on November 14-15 at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY. What's a hackathon, you ask? It's a 24-hour coding maration filled with free food, free swag, good vibes, and an environment perfect for building a project with code! Not yet a programming ninja? No worries, you'll have plenty of people to work with who will encourage you to improve your skills and inspire new ideas.

Don't forget to reserve your spot by registering on their website (hackrpi.com) and RSVP to our event to let us know you're coming!

Coding Nights

Our weekly open coding sessions are the heart and soul of CSS
Wednesdays @ 7pm | Dealy 115

Computer Science Society's open coding nights are casual sessions geared towards collaborative working and helping each other out when we get stuck. We're here to help you leverage the technology out there to make your ideas come to life. Of course, you can also use the awesome space as a great environment for a personal project, classwork, or self-study Feel free to come and go as you please, but be prepared to leave with some (epic) stickers and more than a few inspired ideas.

bitcamp

Fordham University's Computer Science Society is proud to take you to bitcamp, a 36 hour hackathon experience at the University of Maryland!

Rewind, what's a hackathon?

It's an awesome 24-hour coding marathon. It's a great time to get together and collaborate (or not) with other hackers to build something awesome. If you're intimidated by the label "hacker"- don't be. You don't have to be a coding ninja to go to this thing. You just have to be excited and willing to give your best shot at making something cool or simply learning something new.

Read this quick article for a great summary of what goes on at hackathons.

ATTENDANCE IS COMPLETELY FREE AND ALL MEALS ARE PROVIDED! All you have to do is bring a laptop and charger and you're set! You can also show up any point during the day to take part. If you can't make it, we'll be sure to provide the stream for the demos at the end so you can cheer us on from home.

Visit our facebook page here: https://www.facebook.com/events/922996011066383
